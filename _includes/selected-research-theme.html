Agentic Data Scientists

How can agentic data scientists formulate, execute, and improve analytical and scientific workflows while remaining grounded in data, tools, and evidence?

Representative project

EvoDS: Self-Evolving Autonomous Data Science Agent

Theme 02

Grounded Urban Agents

How can urban agents reason with tools, constraints, collaborators, and environmental feedback to make reliable decisions in complex cities?

Representative project

DeepTravel: Autonomous Travel Planning Agents

Training grounded planning agents through a realistic tool sandbox, hierarchical feedback, and experience replay. KDD 2026.

Related: USTBench · CoLLMLight

Representative project

UniExtreme: A Universal Foundation Model for Extreme Weather

Learning transferable representations of complex, evolving environmental systems. KDD 2026.
