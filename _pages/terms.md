## Privacy Policy

This site does not use advertising trackers or build personal visitor profiles.

### Privacy-friendly analytics

This site uses [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/) for aggregate traffic analytics and to display the site-wide visit count in the footer. It records visited pages, referring sites, broad location, browser and system information, language, and screen size. These statistics help me understand which research and publication pages are useful and whether the site works well across devices.

GoatCounter does not use cookies or persistent visitor identifiers. IP addresses and user-agent information may be processed temporarily to distinguish visits, but are not stored in its analytics database. The resulting statistics are aggregated and are not used for advertising.
